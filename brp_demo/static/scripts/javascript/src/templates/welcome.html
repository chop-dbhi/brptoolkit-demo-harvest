Welcome!

If this is your first time, welcome to Harvest! This query system allows you to search through a variety of different data pertaining to our biorepository. Below is a list of helpful tips to get you started and a description of the different categories available for exploration.
  • Get started by searching or browsing through the menu on the left side of available clinical data and specimen type filters.
  • Once you have identified the data filter you would like to use for your query choose Apply Filters.
  • Next choose View Results to see your detailed report
  • On the results page you can add data points to your query by selecting Change Columns.
  • You can Save or Export the data from your queries for future use
